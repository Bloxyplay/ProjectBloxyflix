About BloxyFlix

Terms of Service Privacy Policy About

BloxyFlix is a free educational digital archive dedicated to preserving and providing access to North Korean cinema, cultural performances, documentaries, and live broadcasts. Our mission is to make these important cultural artifacts available for educational and research purposes worldwide.

Our Mission

We believe in the power of cultural exchange and understanding. North Korean cinema and media represent a unique and often underexplored aspect of global film history. By providing accessible, organized access to this content, we aim to:

  • Support academic research into North Korean media, culture, and society
  • Preserve cultural content that might otherwise be lost or difficult to access
  • Foster greater cross-cultural understanding through media accessibility
  • Provide a resource for educators, students, and researchers worldwide

What We Offer

📺 Video Archive

Our growing collection includes:

  • North Korean Films — Classic and contemporary cinema from the DPRK
  • Cultural Performances — Music, dance, and theatrical productions
  • Documentaries — Historical and educational content
  • News Broadcasts — KCTV daily news archives
  • YouTube Archives — Curated content from verified sources

📡 Live Streams

Access live broadcasts including:

  • Korean Central Television (KCTV) international feed
  • KCBS Radio
  • Voice of Korea
  • Pyongyang FM

Content Sources

We source our content from publicly available archives and verified channels:

  • Korean Central Television (KCTV) — Official international broadcasts
  • Intchoson — Streaming platform archives
  • Bloxy Play TV — Our sister streaming service
  • YouTube — Verified archival channels including Meari, Intchoson
  • Public Cultural Archives — Official state performances and events

About Bloxy Play

BloxyFlix is a project of Bloxy Play Technology & Blocky Studio Company Limited, a technology company focused on media archiving and streaming services.

Bloxy Play TV, our sister platform, is a dedicated streaming service for Korean Central Television LIVE broadcasts and archives, featuring a modern interface with multi-language support.

Our Commitment

We are committed to:

  • Remaining Free — BloxyFlix will always be free to use
  • Privacy First — We don't collect or store personal data
  • Educational Focus — Content is provided for research and learning
  • Continuous Improvement — Regular updates and new content additions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BloxyFlix. Can't find your answer? Contact us.

General
What is BloxyFlix?
BloxyFlix is a free, forever educational streaming service dedicated to North Korean cinema, cultural performances, documentaries, live television, and international TV channels. It is operated by the Bloxy Play Team and sourced from publicly available archives.
Is BloxyFlix free to use?
Yes — completely free, forever. There are no subscriptions, no hidden fees, no paid tiers, and no advertisements. BloxyFlix will always remain a free service for education and research.
Who runs BloxyFlix?
BloxyFlix is created and maintained by the Bloxy Play Team — the same team behind Bloxy Play TV, an informational streaming service for Korean Central Television archives and live broadcasts. It is owned and operated by Bloxy Play Technology & Blocky Studio Company Limited.
What kind of content is on BloxyFlix?
BloxyFlix hosts North Korean movies, cultural and artistic performances, documentaries, news reports, YouTube archives, live radio and television streams (KCTV, KCBS, Voice of Korea, Pyongyang FM), and an international TV channels section with more channels coming soon.
Is this content legal to watch?
All content on BloxyFlix is sourced from publicly available archives and streams, including KCTV's official international broadcast, Intchoson, KoryoTV, and YouTube. The platform is strictly for educational and research purposes. Users are responsible for ensuring compliance with local laws in their region.
Watching & Playback
Why won't a video play?
Some streams may be temporarily offline or geo-restricted in your region. Try refreshing the page or checking your internet connection. For live streams, the channel may not be broadcasting at the moment. YouTube-sourced videos require a stable connection and may be unavailable if the source video has been removed.
How do I use Theater Mode?
While a video is playing, press the T key on your keyboard to toggle Theater Mode. This expands the player to fill the screen for a more immersive experience. Press T again or Esc to exit.
What are the keyboard shortcuts?
Space / K — Play/Pause · F — Fullscreen · T — Theater Mode · M — Mute · Arrow Up/Down — Volume · Arrow Left/Right or J/L — Seek 10 seconds · / — Open Search · Esc — Close overlays. You can also view the full list via Profile → Keyboard Shortcuts.
Can I change the playback speed?
Yes. Go to Settings and use the Playback Speed selector to choose from 0.5×, 0.75×, 1× (Normal), 1.25×, 1.5×, or 2×. Your preference is saved automatically for future sessions.
Does BloxyFlix support Picture-in-Picture?
Yes, Picture-in-Picture (PiP) is supported on most modern browsers and Android devices. You can enable it from the player controls or via Settings. Note that PiP is not available on all browsers — Safari on iOS has limited support.
Account & Data
Do I need an account to watch?
No account is required. BloxyFlix is completely open — just open the app and start watching. Your watchlist, history, and settings are stored locally on your device using your browser's storage.
Where is my data stored?
All personal data — including your watchlist, watch history, comments, playback settings, and display name — is stored locally on your device using localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server. Your data never leaves your device.
How do I clear my watch history or watchlist?
Go to Settings → Data & Privacy. You'll find buttons to clear your Watch History and Watchlist independently. You can also clear all BloxyFlix data at once by clearing your browser's site data in your device settings.
Can I change my display name?
Yes. Tap the profile icon in the top-right corner to open the profile menu. Your display name is shown at the top — tap it to edit, then click outside to save. The name is used for comments and is stored locally on your device only.
App & Installation
How do I install BloxyFlix on my Android phone?
Open BloxyFlix in Google Chrome on your Android device. You should see an "Install" banner at the bottom of the screen — tap it to add BloxyFlix to your home screen as a Progressive Web App (PWA). Alternatively, tap the browser menu (⋮) and select "Add to Home Screen". Once installed, BloxyFlix launches like a native app with no browser chrome.
Why is PWA only supported on Android?
iOS (iPhone and iPad) has historically had limited and inconsistent PWA support, which caused display and playback bugs in BloxyFlix. To protect the experience for iOS users, the install prompt is only shown on Android for now. iOS support is being investigated and may be added in a future update.
Does the app work offline?
BloxyFlix uses a Service Worker to cache the app shell, so the interface will load offline. However, actual video content and live streams require an active internet connection to play, as media files are served from external sources.
TV Channels & Live Streams
Why is KCTV sometimes not working?
Korean Central Television (KCTV) is streamed via Intchoson's official international feed. Occasional outages can occur due to scheduled maintenance, satellite downtime, or broadcast interruptions from the source. If the stream is down, please try again later — it usually resumes within a few hours.
When will Chinese TV channels (CCTV/CGTN) be available?
CCTV News broadcast archives are now available in the archive — filter by "CCTV" on the home page. Live channel streams for CGTN and additional CCTV channels are still being sourced. Planned channels include CGTN English, Documentary, Arabic, French, Spanish, Russian, CCTV-4, and CCTV-News.
What is Bloxy Play TV?
Bloxy Play TV is a streaming platform built by the Bloxy Play Team, inspired by Intchoson but with a more polished interface featuring liquid glass effects, blur effects, sound waves, and multi-language support. BloxyFlix is a companion archive service from the same team. You can find Bloxy Play TV at bloxyplaytv.netlify.app.
How can I report a broken stream or suggest a feature?
You can contact the Bloxy Play Team via email at [email protected]. We welcome feedback on broken streams, missing content, feature requests, and general suggestions. We read every message and aim to respond promptly.

🇬🇧 English

Due to China's Great Firewall (GFW) internet censorship system, some of the international streaming services, video hosting platforms, and content delivery networks (CDNs) used by BloxyFlix may not be accessible from mainland China. This includes YouTube embedded videos, certain .m3u8 live stream URLs, and MP4 files hosted on overseas servers.

Solution: Using a reliable VPN (Virtual Private Network) tool to connect to an overseas server will allow you to access all BloxyFlix content normally. We recommend choosing a VPN service that works in China, such as ExpressVPN, NordVPN, or protocols like Shadowsocks. Please ensure your usage complies with local regulations.

Note on Live Channels: The upcoming CCTV / CGTN channels will have their stream addresses specifically optimised for mainland China users — stay tuned!

Myanmar currently faces significant internet infrastructure challenges, including inconsistent connection speeds, frequent service interruptions, and network throttling in some regions. These issues can cause BloxyFlix videos to buffer heavily, fail to load, or disconnect mid-stream.

Tips for Myanmar users:
• Lower the quality — go to Settings and select 480p or lower to reduce buffering on slow connections.
• Use Wi-Fi when available — mobile data in many areas is too slow for live streams; fixed broadband or fibre works better.
• Try YouTube-sourced videos first — they are hosted on Google's servers which tend to have better local caching in Myanmar than our direct MP4 streams.
